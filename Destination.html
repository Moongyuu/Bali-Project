Lovina
Located on the north coast of the Indonesian island of Bali, is a stunning beach destination that offers an experience unlike any other in Bali, Lovina is renowed for its tranquil natural beauty, relaxing atmosphere, and the diversity of activities that visitors can enjoy.
Nusa Penida
Take a speed boat from Sanur to one of Bali’s most enchanting locations. This spectacular island is outlined by towering limestone cliffs and surrounded by azure waters.
Ubud
Set amid jungle ravines and rolling emerald foothils, Ubud has iong been the centre of Bali’s artisanal traditions and spiritual life.
Sanur
The postcard-perfect beaches of Sanur are truly idyllic, the realization of all those daydreams about Bali beach vacations. Sanur is the type of place where hip Europeans who have outgrown the party scene come to recharge.
Jimbaran
One of the beaches in the south side of the Kuta. For you who are on holiday with family, this beach is perfect for you both. While enjoying sunset, you can have dinner on the beach in a very romantic atmosphere, like candle light dinner on the beach.
Sidemen
If you want local villages, rice terraces, amazing views, and a quiet place away from the modern world, then visiting Sidemen, Bali, is a no-brainer.
